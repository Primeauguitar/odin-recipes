Coffeerubbed Ribeye

Description

Easy grill food

Ingredients

  • Ribeye steaks
  • Ground coffee
  • Brown sugar
  • Kosher salt
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Mix the spices all together until completely combined.
  2. Pour dry rub over steak
  3. Rub spice mix into meat
  4. Let rest covered for 30 minutes at room temperature
  5. Grill on high heat for about 4-6 minutes per side, or until it reaches an internal temperature of about 130 for medium-rare
  6. Remove from the grill and let sit covered in foil for about 10 minutes
